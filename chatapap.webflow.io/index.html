What is LuminaAI

LuminaAI is a chatbot developed by Code Clan Team 1 Led by Omijeh David Odianonsen 👾.
It is an extremely versatile chatbot that is built to be able to be a user's companion and satisfy a user's interests.

Is it safe to use LuminaAI?

Yes, it is extremely safe to use LuminaAI, your data and chat history are completely private even to us.

Do i have to pay to use LuminaAI?

LuminaAI is completely free, we have no plans of monetising it anytime soon!

What makes LuminaAI unique from other chatbots?

LuminaAI is an extremely versatile chatbot and although it may not be as smart as chatgpt 4.0, its can perform extremely complex tasks and analysis.
